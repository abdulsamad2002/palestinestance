# Palestine Stance

An AI-powered database that tracks public figures' stances on the Palestine cause. Search any celebrity to instantly discover their position with verified sources and confidence ratings.

## About

Palestine Stance is a transparent, crowd-powered platform that helps people make informed decisions about which public figures they support. By leveraging artificial intelligence and web research, the platform automatically discovers and verifies celebrities' public statements and actions regarding Palestine.

### Key Features

- **Real-Time AI Research**: Search for any celebrity and get instant results. If they're not in our database, our AI researches their stance in real-time using credible sources from across the web.

- **Verified Information**: Every entry includes multiple credible sources (news articles, verified social media posts, official statements) with confidence scores showing the reliability of the data.

- **Comprehensive Database**: Automatically growing database of public figures including actors, musicians, athletes, politicians, and other prominent personalities.

- **Transparent Sourcing**: All information is sourced from reputable outlets. Every claim links back to its original source for verification.

- **Hall of Fame**: Highlights the most prominent voices supporting Palestine, ranked by verification confidence and source quality.

### How It Works

1. **Search**: Enter any celebrity's name in the search bar
2. **AI Research**: If not in the database, AI researches their public stance using credible sources (takes 5-10 seconds)
3. **Verification**: AI analyzes sources for credibility and assigns a confidence score
4. **Results**: View their stance (Pro-Palestine, Neutral, or Pro-Israel) with all supporting sources
5. **Database Growth**: Every search automatically adds to the database for faster future lookups

### Stance Categories

- **Pro-Palestine**: Public figures who have publicly supported Palestine through statements, actions, or donations
- **Neutral/Silent**: Public figures with no clear public position or who have remained silent on the issue
- **Pro-Israel**: Public figures who have publicly supported Israel

### Technology

Built with modern web technologies to ensure fast, reliable, and accurate information delivery:

- **Next.js 14**: React framework for server-side rendering and optimal performance
- **AI Research**: Powered by Groq's Llama 3.3 70B model for intelligent web research
- **MongoDB Atlas**: Cloud database for scalable storage and fast queries
- **Real-time Processing**: Searches are cached for instant subsequent lookups

### Why Palestine Stance?

In an era where public figures' voices carry significant influence, it's important to know where they stand on critical humanitarian issues. Palestine Stance makes this information:

- **Accessible**: Simple search interface, no account required
- **Verifiable**: Every claim backed by credible sources
- **Up-to-date**: AI research ensures current information
- **Transparent**: Full source attribution and confidence scores
- **Unbiased**: Automated AI research reduces human bias

### Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals with factual, sourced information about public figures' positions on Palestine, enabling them to make informed decisions about who they support, which voices they amplify, and how they engage with celebrities on this critical humanitarian issue.
